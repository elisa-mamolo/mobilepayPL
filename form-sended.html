About MobilePay How to register What can you do

To register, all you need is:

1. A Polish mobilephonenumber
2. PESEL
3. A paying card from a Polish Bank – Visa, Mastercard, Maestro or Visa Electorn
4. A bank account in Poland
5. A valid email address

To get started with Mobilepay, you shall:

• Type in your name and PESEL
• Read and accept the terms and conditions
• Confirm your mobile number with an activation code sent by SMS
• Type in your card number and bank account details
• Create your own password

After all this, you will log in to MobilePay with a password you’ve created yourself.
Once you’ve registered yourself as a user, you can:

• Send money to anybody with a Polish mobile number
• Pay in shops that work with MobilePay
• Receive money from anybody with a Polish mobile number
• Share a bill with your friends
• See a list of all your transactions
• Have a clear overview of your spendings
• Pay safely when purchasing from webshops.
