FAQ

If you haven't find a solution for your problem you can contact us and we will glad to help you solving your issues

Can I use the app if I am not a Danske Bank client?
Yes, if you are a client of one of our partners, you can find the list If you want to have a bank account with us, visit our branch bank in your town or contact us.
Do i need a special bank account to have access to this service?
No, you have to be a Danske Bank or partner client with any kind of bank account. This service is completely free to use.
How much does this service cost?
MobilePay is free and it will always be free and secure.
Do i need a special phone to use the app?
You need to use a smartphone running Android or IOS to have access to the app, moreover you have to be connected to wifi or 3G
Where can I download the app?
MobilePay app is avaiable for free on Apple and Google store for free.
How much does each transaction cost?
MobilePay transactions are free.
Can I transfer money everywhere and to everyone?
You can transfer money to Polish clients which are using the same app. Now the service is avaiable just in Poland, Denmark and Norway but is not still possible to send money to another country.
I have a problem, can I contact the customer service by phone?
MobilePay deliver high end customer service, here is a link to our contacts.
How many money min/max can I tranfer?
There is no limit for any transaction, remember that you can always ask for a receipt.
Is possible to have a receipt for a transaction?
MobilePay offer you the possibility to request the receipt after each transaction.
I can't open the app, what should I do?
MobilePay cares about your security and we have probably just released a new update. We suggest you to download the update and try again.
Can I trust the transactions?
MobilePay is build on a completely secure platform, our engineers are always working to deliver a secure product. New updates are released when needed and we suggest to install them as soon as possible.
I've contact the customer service but I haven't received an answer yet.
MobilePay customer service will contact you in 48 hours delivering a custom solution for your problem.
I changed my number, what do I have to do?
Open the app and enter your login informations, go on setting section under the mobile number menu you can find the text "change my number". After tapping on it you just have to follow the instructions and use again the app.
I sent the wrong amount of money!
MobilePay customer service is available to assist you, you can contact us.
I blocked my card can I use MobilePay?
Unfortunately you can't use MobilePay until your card is unlocked. If you are having any problems contact our customer service
